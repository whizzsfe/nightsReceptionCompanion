Shift Motivation
You are the heart of this hotel tonight — keep going.

Excellence Tips
🌟
Own Every Moment
Night guests have fewer people to turn to. When you solve a problem at 2am, you become the most important person in their stay.
📞
Phone Etiquette
Answer within 3 rings. Greet warmly, state the hotel name, your name, and ask how you can help. End every call with a positive close.
🧘
Stay Calm Under Pressure
Take a breath before responding to any difficult situation. A measured, confident response defuses 90% of escalations before they start.
🗒️
Document Everything
If it happened on your shift, log it. Clear, timely records protect you, your team, and the guest — even if nothing comes of it.
📤
Handover Excellence
A great handover takes 5 minutes and saves the next shift hours of confusion. Leave notes you'd want to receive yourself.
✨
The Little Extras
A welcome note, remembering a name, pre-empting a need — these are the moments guests describe to friends. You create them.
